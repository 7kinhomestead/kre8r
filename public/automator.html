Setup — First Time
1
Close all Chrome windows, then relaunch Chrome with remote debugging enabled:
chrome.exe --remote-debugging-port=9222 --user-data-dir="%LOCALAPPDATA%\Google\Chrome\RemoteDebug"
2
Log into app.kajabi.com in that Chrome window.
3
Click Connect Chrome above. Playwright will attach to your existing session — no credentials stored.
