Hey [first name] — One thing I didn't cover in the solar video — and I should have: The single biggest mistake people make when sizing a solar system is designing for what they use now, not what they'll use in 5 years. Your homestead grows. Your energy needs grow with it. If you build tight to your current usage, you're going to be adding panels and batteries on the back end — which is always more expensive than getting it right the first time. Here's the quick math I use: take your current average daily usage, multiply by 1.5, and design to that number.
